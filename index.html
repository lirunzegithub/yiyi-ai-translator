隐私政策

译亿AI（以下简称“本应用”）是一款 iOS 端的情景式 / 对话式翻译工具。我们高度重视你的隐私。 本应用没有任何自有服务器，开发者不收集、不存储、不上传你的任何个人数据。

1. 我们如何处理你的数据

  • 文字 / 图片翻译：默认由设备本地的离线模型与词典处理（翻译模式的文字翻译始终在本地完成，不会外发）。若你在「设置」里自行配置了第三方 API（OpenAI 兼容接口），则助手模式的对话内容（文字及必要的上下文）与图片翻译中的图片及随附文字会发送到你自己填写的那个 API 地址，仅用于生成译文/回答。每个 API 服务首次发送前，应用会弹窗说明将发送的内容与接收方，征得你的明确同意后才发送；你可随时在应用内「设置 → 隐私」撤回该授权。该数据由你选择的 API 提供方按其隐私政策处理，与本应用开发者无关。
  • 语音识别：录音、转写与说话人分离全部在设备本地完成（端侧模型），录音与转写内容不会上传到任何服务器；转写出的文字再走上面的本地翻译流程。
  • 位置信息：仅在你授权后获取。用于：①每次发送时把位置记入仅保存在本机的「安全与记录」行程日志（供你自查）；②（可选）把粗略经纬度发送给天气服务以显示当地天气，城市名由 Apple 系统地理编码服务解析。位置信息不会发送给任何 AI 服务商。
  • 会话历史：你的对话、录音、图片仅保存在设备本地（iOS SwiftData / 沙盒），可随时删除。
  • API 密钥：你填写的第三方 API 密钥仅加密保存在设备的钥匙串（Keychain），不外传。

2. 第三方服务

为实现功能，本应用可能与以下服务通信（仅传输实现该功能所必需的数据）：

  • 你自行配置的 AI 服务提供方（可选）：仅在你主动配置并逐服务同意后，接收助手模式对话与图片翻译内容。接收方完全由你选择。
  • Open-Meteo（天气，可选）：仅接收粗略经纬度，不含任何身份信息。
  • Apple 系统服务：地理编码（坐标→城市名）由 iOS 系统完成，遵循 Apple 隐私政策。
  • Hugging Face（可选）：仅在你主动下载本地模型时访问，用于下载模型文件，不上传你的任何数据。

本应用只向上述第三方传输实现对应功能所必需的最少数据。这些第三方各有其隐私政策；配置自定义 AI 服务前，请确认其数据保护水平满足你的要求。

3. 我们不做的事

  • 不使用任何广告 SDK、第三方分析/统计 SDK。
  • 不使用 IDFA、不进行跨 App 跟踪。
  • 不为开发者收集或回传任何数据（本应用无后端）。

4. 你的控制权

  • 可在应用内「设置 → 隐私」随时撤回对第三方 AI 服务的数据共享授权；撤回后再次发送前会重新征求同意。删除或停用 API 配置后，全部处理即回到纯本地。
  • 可在 iOS「设置 → 译亿AI」中随时撤销麦克风、定位、相册、语音识别等权限。
  • 可在应用内删除任意会话/录音/图片。
  • 无需注册账号，卸载应用即清除本地数据。

5. 儿童

本应用非面向 13 岁以下儿童设计，不会有意收集儿童个人信息。

6. 变更

隐私政策如有更新，将在本页面更新生效日期。

Privacy Policy

译亿AI (“the App”) is an iOS situational / conversational translation tool. We take your privacy seriously. The App has no servers of its own; the developer does not collect, store, or upload any of your personal data.

1. How we handle your data

  • Text / image translation: handled by on-device offline models and dictionaries by default (text translation in Translate mode always runs locally and is never sent out). If you configure a third-party API (OpenAI-compatible) in Settings, then Assistant-mode conversations (text plus necessary context) and images with accompanying text in image translation are sent to the API endpoint you yourself entered, solely to produce translations/answers. Before the first send to each API service, the App shows a dialog explaining what will be sent and to whom, and sends only with your explicit consent; you can revoke it anytime in the App under Settings → Privacy. That data is handled by the API provider you chose under their own privacy policy; it does not reach the App’s developer.
  • Speech recognition: recording, transcription, and speaker separation all run on-device; audio and transcripts are never uploaded to any server. The transcribed text then follows the local translation flow above.
  • Location: accessed only with your permission, to: (a) log each send’s location into a trip journal stored only on your device (for your own review); (b) optionally send approximate coordinates to a weather service to show local weather, with city names resolved by Apple’s system geocoding. Location is never sent to any AI provider.
  • Conversation history: your chats, recordings, and images are stored only on your device (iOS SwiftData / sandbox) and can be deleted anytime.
  • API keys: any third-party API key you enter is stored encrypted in the device Keychain only and never transmitted elsewhere.

2. Third-party services

To provide features, the App may communicate with (transmitting only what that feature requires):

  • Your configured AI service provider (optional): receives Assistant-mode conversations and image-translation content only after you configure it and give per-service consent. The recipient is entirely your choice.
  • Open-Meteo (weather, optional): receives approximate coordinates only, with no identifying information.
  • Apple system services: geocoding (coordinates → city name) is performed by iOS under Apple’s privacy policy.
  • Hugging Face (optional): accessed only when you actively download an on-device model; used solely to download model files, never to upload your data.

The App transmits only the minimum data each feature requires. Each third party has its own privacy policy; before configuring a custom AI service, please confirm its data protection meets your expectations.

3. What we do NOT do

  • No advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No IDFA, no cross-app tracking.
  • No data collected or sent back to the developer (the App has no backend).

4. Your controls

  • Revoke your data-sharing consent for third-party AI services anytime in the App under Settings → Privacy; the App will ask again before the next send. Removing or disabling the API configuration returns all processing fully on-device.
  • Revoke microphone, location, photos, and speech-recognition permissions anytime in iOS Settings.
  • Delete any conversation/recording/image inside the App.
  • No account required; uninstalling removes local data.

5. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date on this page will be updated.
